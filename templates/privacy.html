Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

GetGrowline ("the app", "we", "us") is a YouTube channel analytics tool that lets you look up and visualize publicly available statistics for YouTube channels and videos. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Information we collect

  • Channel/video searches: When you search for a channel, the app sends the channel name or URL you enter to the YouTube Data API to retrieve publicly available statistics (views, subscribers, video counts, publish dates, etc.). We do not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • Local history and preferences: Your recent searches, saved dashboards, and display preferences are stored locally on your device (in your browser's local storage) so the app can restore them. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Optional feedback: If you choose to submit feedback through the app, the message you write — and an email address only if you voluntarily provide one — is sent to us so we can respond and improve the app.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.
  • We do not require sign-in and do not access your personal YouTube or Google account.
  • We do not sell or share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

Third-party services

The app retrieves public data through the YouTube Data API, a Google service. Your use of YouTube data is also subject to the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy. The app is hosted on Render; standard server logs (such as IP address and request time) may be processed by the hosting provider to operate and secure the service.

Data retention

Locally stored history and preferences remain on your device until you clear your browser data or uninstall the app. Feedback messages are retained only as long as needed to address your input and improve the product.

Children's privacy

GetGrowline is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can clear your local history and saved dashboards at any time from within the app or by clearing your browser/app storage. Providing feedback and an email address is entirely optional.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at growonlinelabs@gmail.com.
